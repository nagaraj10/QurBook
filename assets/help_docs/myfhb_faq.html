Teleconsultation

What is Teleconsultation?

In simple words, it is remote healthcare consultation given by a certified physician to a patient over the internet through video calls and chats using smartphones and computers.

Will Teleconsultation give a similar experience as Clinic consultation?

All aspects of clinic consultations except for physical examination would be present in teleconsultation, with the added benefit of talking to your doctor from the comfort of your homes. All questions concerning the symptoms, illness, and treatment can be discussed, diagnosed, and prescribed in real-time.

Does myFHB provide telemedicine?

No, as of now, myFHB focuses only on teleconsultation. While telemedicine refers to remote diagnosis and treatment of patients using technology, teleconsultation involves only doctors' online consultation with patients.

Is my teleconsultation private and secure?

Absolutely! myFHB is built on a robust and secure infrastructure. Your teleconsultation is completely secure, private, and compliant with dynamic healthcare regulations. Your digitized health records and device readings are stored in a centralized digital repository exclusive for your family.

Are all consultations appointment-based?

Yes! you can schedule an appointment with the doctor of your choice and receive their consultation.

Will the fee be refunded if the doctor cancels my teleconsultation appointment?

Yes, it will be refunded 100%.

Will I be able to reschedule an appointment?

Yes, but you can only reschedule before the start of the appointment.

Will there be a cancellation fee if I cancel the appointment?

We understand there are times where you had to cancel your appointment. But it also means you may be preventing another patient from getting that much-needed consultation. If you cancel an appointment, you may incur a cancellation fee.

Can I have a record of my past appointment discussions with the doctor?

Yes, discussions made through chats can be accessed, but the platform will provide no audio/video recordings.

Would I be able to add a family member, who does not own a phone?

How do I move family records from one person to another?

Long press on the desired record. A 'Move to' icon would appear. Select the icon to move the record to another family member. Alternatively, when you capture the record, there is an option to switch the record to a family member.

How do I make a doctor as a 'My Preferred' (Primary) doctor?

Under the 'Doctors List', you could see the doctor's names. Long press on the corresponding doctor's name. An alert will be displayed to 'confirm' your action of changing/setting the preferred doctor. Upon confirmation, you will be able to set your preferred doctor.

Do I have to pay for using myFHB?

myFHB is a free app, and there are no hidden charges for storing or accessing health records and invoking Sheela – personalized healthcare assistant. However, standard charges will apply for accessing the Teleconsultation feature in the app.

What type of documents/records can I store in myFHB?

You can store prescriptions, device readings, medical reports, lab reports, bills, ID docs, and any health records. For your convenience, you can also have voice notes for documents/records.

Can I share myFHB documents to users outside of my 'Approved Users' list?

For security and privacy reasons, within myFHB, you would be able to share only with the 'Approved Users' list. However, if you wish to share with other users, you could download the documents/records into your phone and share it externally from local storage.

Can I set the Default Provider (hospital/doctor/clinic/lab/pharmacy) to my entire family?

No. Default/Preferred Provider has to be set individually for every family member.

Can my provider (hospital/doctor/clinic/lab/pharmacy) directly send my health records to myFHB?

Yes, we are continually working to integrate direct information sharing from providers to our patients. If you do not see your provider (who wants to share data with you) listed, please contact us, and we will be able to help you.

How long will myFHB store my family records?

myFHB will store all family health records for as long as you are associated with it. If the myFHB account remains dormant, records will be saved for up to 6 months from the last date of use.

Can I refer my friends and families to myFHB?

Of course, all you have to do is go to the myFHB profile in the play store, and you can see the menu icon at the top right. Tap it and click 'share' in various social media apps such as Whatsapp, Gmail, Twitter, Facebook, etc.

Could someone outside of my family add me as a member of their myFHB account?

Yes, it is possible. They could do so by entering your phone number in their myFHB account. A verification OTP would be sent to your phone. If you share the OTP with them, they could add you as a member of their myFHB family.

Will I know who would view my health records within myFHB?

Absolutely! You will have complete control of who gets to view your health information. Any viewer could view your record only after sharing the OTP received on your phone with them.

Can someone request access to view myFHB records?

Yes, anybody who has your phone number can request to view your records, but you will have to share the OTP received on your phone with them, as a way of acknowledging and accepting to share your records with them.

What wearables' data can I integrate with myFHB?

The myFHB supports integration with Google Fit and Apple HealthKit wearables.
